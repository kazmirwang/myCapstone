Top Elegible Batchelorae

  1. Spinellus fusiger

    • Common name: Bonnet Mold
    • Simple brown-grey structure
    • Enjoys other decaying fungi

  2. Cladosporium conidia

    • Common name: Common Mold
    • Small particulate spores
    • Found almost everywhere

  3. Penicilium chrysogenum

    • Common name: Penicillin
    • Dry bush-shaped spores
    • Used to make antibiotics
